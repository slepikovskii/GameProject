What is game about:
	This is a game for children of 7-10 years old teaching them to be tidy and clean their classrooms at school and sort garbage.

It is for PC users. 

How to run the game:
	hotkeys:
		W - go forward
		S - go backward
		A - turn left
		D - turn right
		Space - jump
		E - pick up
